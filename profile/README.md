# Kyberdyne White Paper 


---

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW: What is Kyberdyne? 

Kyberdyne is a **DBG (Deck-Building Game)** with a **Rogue-like mechanism** built by an AAA game studio. It brings high-quality gaming elements to blockchain players, allowing them to build dream teams and earn unique rewards.

* **Gameplay Modes:** Players use NFT Characters (Heroes) for **PVE** (endless fun and idle rewards) and **PVP** (season rewards).


* **Economic Model:** Kyberdyne is a real **F2P (Free-to-Play), P2E (Play-to-Earn)** game.



---

2. GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY 

The team focuses on highly-immersive gameplay and continuous content mining.

* **Social Channels:** TikTok, Instagram, Telegram, Twitter, and Discord for announcements and event spreading.


* **Engagement:** AMAs, giveaways, and airdrops.


* 
**Content:** Instructions and user guides on Medium, YouTube, and TikTok.



---

3. PRODUCT VIABILITY 

First Metaverse Based DBG 

Created by the world's 1st metaverse game engine, the game offers high rendering power.

* **Customization:** Players can customize character appearance (virtual doppelgängers), progression paths, gender, race, and storylines.


* **User-Centric:** Everything is based on user creation and experience.



Fun Compelling Gameplay 

Strategies are designed pre-battle and in-battle through card combinations. The Rogue-like mechanism ensures every adventure is a new experience.

Play-to-Earn & Dynamic NFTs 

* **Sustainable Economy:** Players can enter for free to earn NFTs and **$KBD tokens**.


* **Unique Generation:** Cards are generated by an **AI algorithm**, making each unique.


* **Minting:** Once cards reach **3-star level**, they can be minted into NFTs.


* **Dynamic Attributes:** NFTs upgrade based on hero experience, with all attribute changes stored permanently on-chain.



---

4. WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF KYBERDYNE 

Set in a **cyber-wasteland** post-apocalyptic era following the collapse of a cyberpunk civilization due to war and plague. Surviving creatures have formed various races:

| Race | Description | Attributes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Mutant** | Controversial origins; horrifying creatures. | Unstable attack output; extremely high max attack. |
| **Cyborg** | Retrofitted via biochemical technology.| Proficient in everything; reflected in card strength. |
| **Electronic** | People with over 60% prosthetization. | Strongest bodies; versatile in any team position. |
| **Android** | Advanced AI assistants. | Loyal and most reliable partners. |

---

5. TOKENOMICS 

#### Token Economy Flow

**$KBD Token Utility:**

* **In-game Energy:** Needed for discovery in PVE.


* **NFT Fuel:** Required to upgrade, synthesize, and breed NFTs.


* **PVP Betting:** Used to bet in arenas and rewarded to winners.



**Earning $KBD and NFTs:**

* **Staking:** Stake $KBD to receive airdropped card fragments.


* **NFT Mining:** Garrison conquered chapters with NFTs to earn $KBD.


* **PVE/PVP:** Earn through active gameplay.
